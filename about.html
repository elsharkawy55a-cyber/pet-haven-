Our Story

Pet Haven was born from a simple belief: pets deserve the very best. Founded in 2020 by a small team of passionate animal lovers in Cairo, we started with a single goal — to make high-quality pet products accessible and easy to order across Egypt.

What started as a weekend passion project quickly grew into a trusted name for thousands of pet owners. We carefully curate every product in our catalog, testing it with our own pets before it ever reaches yours.

Today, Pet Haven offers over 24 premium products across 11 categories — all orderable in seconds via WhatsApp. Because your pet's happiness shouldn't require a complicated checkout process.

Our Journey

From a tiny dream to a growing family

2020 – The Beginning

Pet Haven is founded

Two friends with three cats and two dogs between them decided Egypt's pets deserved better. Pet Haven launched with 5 products on WhatsApp.

2021 – First Expansion

We hit 100 customers

Word of mouth spread fast. We expanded to 12 product categories and began same-day delivery in Cairo and Giza.

2022 – Going National

Delivery across Egypt

Pet Haven started shipping to Alexandria, Mansoura, Aswan, and beyond. Our catalog grew to 24+ curated products.

2023 – Digital Launch

Our website goes live

We launched pethaven.eg to make browsing easier, while keeping our beloved WhatsApp ordering system at the heart of the experience.

2024–Now – Growing Family

500+ happy pets served

Today we serve hundreds of loving pet owners across Egypt, with a team of passionate animal lovers dedicated to your pet's happiness.

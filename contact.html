Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I place an order? ▼
Simply browse our products, click the "Order on WhatsApp" button on any product card, and a WhatsApp message with the product name will open automatically. Just send it and we'll confirm your order!
Do you deliver across all of Egypt? ▼
Yes! We deliver nationwide across Egypt. Delivery in Cairo and Giza is typically same-day or next-day. Other governorates usually take 2–4 business days.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept cash on delivery, Vodafone Cash, Instapay, and bank transfers. We'll confirm the payment method that works best for you when you place your order on WhatsApp.
Can I return a product? ▼
Yes! We offer hassle-free returns within 7 days of delivery for unopened products. Contact us on WhatsApp with your order details and we'll arrange the return quickly.
Are your products authentic and safe for pets? ▼
Absolutely. We only source from trusted, authorized distributors. All products are 100% authentic from reputable brands like Royal Canin, Hill's, Kong, and more. Your pet's safety is our top priority.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Cairo & Giza: same day or next day. Alexandria & major cities: 1–2 days. Other areas: 2–4 business days. We'll give you an exact estimate when you place your order.
